# car ownership

## dealership revenue

- revenue source
    - 36% new
    - 48% used
    - 10% PS & CR
    - 6% financing and insurance
- gross profit
    - 21% new
    - 14% used
    - 32% PS & CR
    - 33% financing and insurance

## sticker prices / MSRP

- always negotiable
- pay attention to the **factory invoice** cost
    - find with sites like unhaggle
- aim for between MSRP and factory invoice cost

## holdback

dealer receives a percentage (~2-3%) from the factory for a sale

## extra products

- extended warranty - not usually worth it
- rust proofing - inflated price

## negotiating 

- tell dealer as little as possible
- negotiate for an all inclusive price

## new vs used

- new cars depreciate faster than used
