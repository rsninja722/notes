# pascal's triangle

related to binomial theorem: $(x+y)^n = \sum_{k=0}^{n} \binom{n}{k} x^k y^{n-k}$

&&&1&&4&&6&&4&&1&&\\\\

&1&&6&&15&&20&&15&&6&&1
\end{matrix}$$
