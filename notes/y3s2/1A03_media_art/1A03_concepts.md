## digital files

- **CODEC** - compression decompression
    - standard for encoding data into a file format, and how to read data from the file format

## digital audio

- **analog signal** - smooth curve
- **digital signal** - discrete samples
    - **sample rate** - samples per second, measured in kHz
        - common rates: CDs - 44.1 kHz, DVDs - 48kHz, high quality - 96kHz, 192kHz
    - **bit depth** - bits used to represent each sample, commonly 8-bit,16-bit
    - **bit rate** - transfer/playback speed
        - common rates: CDs - 1411 kbps, highest mp3 encoding - 320 kbps, streaming - 192/256 kbps, spoken audio - 96/128/160 kbps
    - uncompressed audio CODECs - WAV, AIFF, PCM, DSD
    - lossless audio CODECs - FLAC, ALAC, WMA
    - lossy audio CODECs - MP3, ACC, OGG

## graphic design

- **design** 
    - create, fashion, execute, or construct according to a plan
    - a preliminary sketch or outline showing the main features of something to be executed
- **graphic design** - creation of the visual conditions for making meaning
    - form + content = meaning
    - aesthetics - communicating visually through images, typography, color, shape, line, texture...
    - ideas - a visual language for communicating concepts

### world view

- **world view** - how we perceive and apprehend the world around us, from a specific standpoint
    - individual or societal orientation
    - how we interpret the world
- provides us with context/filter for extracting meaning
- design is created through the world-view lenses of their creator
    - their world view is imparted on the designed object/artifact
- some attempts to reflect a world view fail terribly (pepsi ad) 

### interpretation

- all graphic design is consumed through our world view
- **one measure of success** - can the viewer interpret the message being communicated
- **shared understanding** - regardless of personal world views, many understand and share certain codes and conventions 
- **norms** - design seeks to reflect or refute normative beliefs
    - can appeal or repel particular sensibilities 

### meaning

- **denotative meaning** - descriptive, what does it show?
- **connotative meaning** - communicative, what does it suggest/imply?

## principles and rules of graphic design

- form and/is content
    - form - structure, design. or arrangement of design
    - content - meaning or message contained in the work

1. have a concept
    - what are you going to say and how will you say it
2. communicate don't decorate
