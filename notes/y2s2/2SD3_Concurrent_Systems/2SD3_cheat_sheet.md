# 2SD3 cheat sheet

## definitions

- **concurrent systems** - systems consisting of several parts acting together
- **sequential** - single thread of control
- **concurrent** - multiple threads of control, can perform multiple computations/ control multiple external activates at the same time
- **model** - a simplified representation of the real world
- **labelled transition system (LTS)** - graphical representation of processes
- **finite state process (FSP)** - algebraic representation of processes
